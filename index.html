Resume Expanded Resume

As a life-long learner, my understanding and practical application of digital technology and artistic expression serve the needs and wants of clients. My skills enable me to advance project initiatives and fulfill objectives while giving me a sense of purpose and accomplishment.

I earned my Bachelor of Science Degree in Graphic Design with an emphasis on Illustration from Viterbo University, located in La Crosse, Wisconsin. My education gave me the foundation to excel at application and website design and development. I enjoy the process of discovery and the practical application of problem-solving. Improving production-related processes is also one of my strengths, while matching individual performance to company goals contributes to the success of every endeavor.
