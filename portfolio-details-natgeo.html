Explorer Mindset App

Based on the Learning Framework from National Geographic, this app contains four sections that guide learners through visualizing themselves as explorers.

About

Concurrently, they learn a topic or investigate a question. The app can be a component of a project-based learning unit or a self-directed enrichment activity as students explore interests and issues. The app contains an explore area where the user can place pins on a map and tag the pins with a photo, title, and description. The app was developed using Ionic and Capacitor and written in React. I had to reverse-engineer several WordPress plugins to create a plugin that would interface to WordPress via API connections.

The Explorer Mindset App facilitates a self-directed enrichment activity guided by a teacher and integrated within a content area. Launching from social-emotional awareness activities customizing an Explorer Mindset (MINDSET menu), learners apply the attitudes and skills of the Learning Framework to plan an exploration of a question, task, topic, or issue (PLAN menu). Next, students implement their plan and explore their topic by mapping it (EXPLORE menu). Students can create descriptive location markers with photos to document their investigation or activities. Finally, students are encouraged to reflect on their exploration by creating field notes (ANALYZE menu).

The App Includes

  • A video library to learn about Explorer Mindset
  • Outdoor journaling activities to practice an Explorer Mindset
  • An interactive tool to reflect on, write, and customize your Explorer Mindset, learning about the attitudes and skills of National Geographic Learning Framework (MINDSET)
  • When completed, learners receive a PDF summary that they can download or send
  • An interactive tool to plan a learning expedition (PLAN) Learners receive a PDF graphic organizer that they can download or send when finished
  • An interactive mapping tool (EXPLORE) to create
  • An interactive field notes tool to craft a reflection of their exploration (ANALYZE)

The Story Behind Explorer Mindset

The National Geographic Society provided funding for the development of the Explorer Mindset App. Kelly Koller, an educator and National Geographic Explorer designed the app to help empower students to connect with their inner-explorer as they learn. To complete this project, Kelly founded a non-profit organization, ExplorerMindset.org.
